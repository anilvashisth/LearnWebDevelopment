Car driving 8th day

Learn how to drive a car if you want to take a left or right turn when car is stop. press clutch and apply 1st gear . try to see vehicle reach right and left . handle revolve and apply race. apply 2nd gear and increase the race. if no way to cross any vehicle apply brake and press clutch. if car is in running speed than apply clutch and increased the gear and race. if no space apply brake and come to 1st gear. if speed breaker and turn with the same time apply clutch and handle properly.
